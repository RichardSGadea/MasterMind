How to play:
Test you mind...

- Choose your nickname and difficulty level:

Beginner -- 4 colors and 10 attempts
Intermediate -- 5 colors and 8 attempts
Advance -- 6 colors and 6 attempts

- Select the colors:

Choose the number of colors according to the difficulty level selected.

- Play:

You must try combinations until you find the solution.
When you press the check button you will see how many colors you have found in the correct spot(marked with purple balls) and how many colors you got right, but in the wrong spot (marked with white balls).
Good luck!
